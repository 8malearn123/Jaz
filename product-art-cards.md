# Product Art Cards — fill in, then tell Claude "done"

Each bar can have ONE "art card" (the بطاقة فنية section on its product page): the artwork
printed on that bar's wrapper. Fill only the bars that have artwork — leave a block blank and
that bar simply won't show an art card. All text is bilingual: give EN and AR for each.

Fields per bar:
  - Artwork title (EN / AR)
  - Artist name (EN / AR)
  - Year
  - Description (EN / AR)
